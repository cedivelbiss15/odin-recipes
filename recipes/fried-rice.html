Fried Rice

Fried rice is an easy and tasty dish that anyone can make, even me.

After work, the last thing you want to do is spend two hours making a meal, fried rice is perfect because it doesn't take very long and tastes good. Also, you can put just about anything in it and it'll work, so feel free to customize this recipes as much as you would like.

So, come with me as I teach you how to make the last recipe you'll ever need.

Ingredients

  • Peanut oil
  • Soy sauce
  • 1 chicken breast
  • 1 egg
  • Diced onions
  • However much rice you would like, I never measure it out.

Steps

  1. Put rice in a pot with water and boil it
  2. Put peanut oil into a pan and heat it up
  3. Cut up chicken breast and add any seasoning you would like
  4. Put chicken into the hot pan, move it around and cook it evenly
  5. At this point, the rice should be done, put it in a bowl and stick it in the fridge
  6. In another pan, scramble your egg
  7. Once the egg and chicken are cooked, get a large wok and add peanut oil and soy sauce to it and heat it up
  8. Add everything to the wok and start stirring, add in onions
  9. You can add more soy sauce if you need to, but once the rice is nice and brown you're done

That's it, fried rice is pretty simple
