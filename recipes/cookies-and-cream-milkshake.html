Cookies and Cream Milkshake

Milkshakes are the best kind of desert, what we are making will not look like the picture. This is not a diner in the '50s.

Life is cruel. But a milkshake is always fun to drink and they taste great. There really isn't much else to say. Today we're gonna shake some milk.

Also, milkshakes are stupid expensive. This is a great way to save like $6.

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Cookies and cream ice cream, whatever brand you like
  • Oreos
  • A blender

Steps

  1. Find the glass you want to drink out of, fill have with milk and pour it into the blender
  2. Eyeball the amount of ice cream, you probably want 1/4 of the cup to be ice cream. Toss it in the blender
  3. Toss a couple of oreos in there
  4. Blend
  5. If it's too thick, blend it again. You want to to be a little thick but also don't want it to be like water. Other than that, you're done.
